About SchoolsInfo

Our Mission

SchoolsInfo is a comprehensive platform designed to help parents and guardians find the perfect school for their children. We understand that choosing the right school is one of the most important decisions you'll make for your child's future.

Features

  • Browse comprehensive school listings with detailed information
  • Advanced search filters by location, facilities, and sports
  • Interactive map with school locations
  • Compare up to 2 schools side by side
  • Save favourite schools for quick access
  • Real-time admission availability status
  • Download detailed school information documents
  • Community feedback and ratings

School Information

Each school listing includes comprehensive details such as maximum intake capacity, facility availability (library, canteen, boarding), sports options, admission status, and downloadable detailed documents. All information is regularly updated to ensure accuracy.

Technology

Built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. The platform uses Google Maps API for interactive mapping and browser LocalStorage for saving user preferences. The application is fully responsive and works seamlessly across all major browsers and devices.
