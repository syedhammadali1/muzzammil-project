4. ADMISSION PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Admission Criteria:
- Age-appropriate placement
- Entrance assessment (Grades 1-12)
- Previous academic records review
- Parent interview

Required Documents:
1. Birth certificate (original and copy)
2. Previous school transcripts
3. Transfer certificate
4. Medical fitness certificate
5. Passport-size photographs (4)
6. Parent/Guardian ID proof
7. Address proof

Application Process:
Step 1: Submit online application form
Step 2: Pay application fee ($50)
Step 3: Attend entrance assessment
Step 4: Parent interview (if applicable)
Step 5: Receive admission decision
Step 6: Complete registration and fee payment

Important Dates:
- Application opens: January 1st
- Early admission deadline: March 15th
- Regular admission deadline: May 31st
- School year begins: August 15th

Fees Structure (Annual):
- Pre-K to Grade 5: $8,000 - $12,000
- Grades 6-8: $14,000 - $16,000
- Grades 9-12: $18,000 - $22,000
- Additional fees: Registration ($500), Development ($2,000), Books & Supplies ($800)

Scholarships Available:
- Merit-based scholarships (up to 50% tuition)
- Need-based financial aid
- Sports scholarships
- Academic excellence awards

Current Admission Status: Available
Seats Available: Limited seats in select grades
