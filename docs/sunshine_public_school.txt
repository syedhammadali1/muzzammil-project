4. ADMISSION PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Admission Criteria:
- Age-appropriate admission
- Basic assessment for higher grades
- Merit-based selection
- Priority for local residents

Required Documents:
1. Birth certificate
2. Previous school records
3. Transfer certificate
4. Medical certificate
5. Passport photos (2)
6. Address proof
7. Parent/Guardian ID

Application Process:
Step 1: Collect application form from school
Step 2: Submit filled form with documents
Step 3: Pay application fee ($25)
Step 4: Attend assessment (if required)
Step 5: Receive admission confirmation
Step 6: Complete fee payment

Important Dates:
- Application period: February - April
- Assessment dates: Announced via notice
- Admission confirmation: By May 15th
- Academic year begins: First week of August

Fees Structure (Annual):
- Kindergarten to Grade 5: $2,000 - $3,000
- Grades 6-8: $3,500 - $4,500
- Grades 9-12: $5,000 - $6,500
- Additional: Registration ($100), Books ($300), Uniform ($200)

Financial Aid:
- Fee concessions for deserving students
- Scholarship programs
- Installment payment options

Current Admission Status: Limited
Limited seats available in selected grades
