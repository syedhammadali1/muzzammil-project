4. ADMISSION PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Admission Criteria:
- Age-appropriate admission
- First-come-first-served basis
- Priority for local residents
- Basic interaction assessment (for older grades)

Required Documents:
1. Birth certificate
2. Vaccination records
3. Medical fitness certificate
4. Previous school records (if applicable)
5. Passport photos (2)
6. Parent/Guardian ID
7. Address proof

Application Process:
Step 1: Obtain application form
Step 2: Submit form with documents
Step 3: Pay registration fee ($50)
Step 4: Attend orientation (parents)
Step 5: Receive admission confirmation
Step 6: Complete enrollment

Important Dates:
- Registration: January - March
- Orientation: April
- Classes begin: Late August

Fees Structure (Annual):
- Pre-K to Kindergarten: $1,500 - $2,000
- Grades 1-3: $2,500 - $3,000
- Grades 4-5: $3,000 - $3,500
- Additional: Registration ($100), Books ($200), Supplies ($150)

Financial Assistance:
- Fee concessions available
- Payment plans
- Scholarships for deserving students

Current Admission Status: Full
Waiting list available for next academic year
