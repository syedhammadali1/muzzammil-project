4. ADMISSION PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Admission Criteria:
- Strong academic record
- Entrance examination
- Previous school transcripts
- Letters of recommendation
- Student and parent interviews
- Extracurricular achievements

Required Documents:
1. Birth certificate
2. Academic transcripts (last 3 years)
3. Transfer certificate
4. Entrance exam scores
5. Letters of recommendation (2)
6. Medical certificate
7. Passport photos (4)
8. Parent/Guardian documents

Application Process:
Step 1: Submit online application
Step 2: Pay application fee ($100)
Step 3: Take entrance examination
Step 4: Submit supporting documents
Step 5: Attend interview
Step 6: Receive admission decision
Step 7: Complete registration

Important Dates:
- Application period: October - January
- Entrance exam: February
- Interviews: March
- Admission decisions: April
- Classes begin: Mid-August

Fees Structure (Annual):
- Grade 9: $12,000
- Grade 10: $13,000
- Grade 11: $14,000
- Grade 12: $15,000
- Additional: Registration ($750), Development ($3,000), Books ($1,000)

Scholarships and Financial Aid:
- Merit-based scholarships (up to 100%)
- Need-based financial aid
- Sports scholarships
- Academic excellence awards

Current Admission Status: Limited
Limited seats available for Grade 9 and Grade 10
