4. ADMISSION PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Admission Criteria:
- Age verification
- Entrance assessment
- Previous academic performance
- Extracurricular achievements
- Parent interview

Required Documents:
1. Birth certificate (original)
2. Academic transcripts (previous 2 years)
3. Transfer certificate
4. Medical fitness certificate
5. Passport photos (6)
6. Parent/Guardian ID and address proof
7. Character certificate from previous school

Application Process:
Step 1: Online or offline application
Step 2: Pay application fee ($75)
Step 3: Schedule entrance test
Step 4: Attend assessment and interview
Step 5: Receive admission decision
Step 6: Complete registration process

Important Dates:
- Applications open: December 1st
- Early bird deadline: February 28th
- Regular deadline: April 30th
- Classes commence: Mid-August

Fees Structure (Annual):
- Pre-School to Grade 5: $6,000 - $9,000
- Grades 6-8: $10,000 - $12,000
- Grades 9-12: $13,000 - $16,000
- Additional: Registration ($500), Development ($2,500), Transport ($1,500)

Scholarships:
- Academic excellence scholarships
- Sports scholarships
- Need-based financial aid
- Sibling discounts

Current Admission Status: Available
Open admissions for most grades
